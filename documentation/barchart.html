Bar Chart

A bar graph can be defined as a graphical representation of data, quantities, or numbers using bars or strips. They are used to compare and contrast different types of data, frequencies, or other measures of distinct categories of data.

Why have we used bar chart?

1.It makes it easy to compare the scores of different groups: A bar chart allows you to compare the scores of different branches easily. You can see at a glance which branches performed the best and which ones performed the worst.

2.It is easy to read and interpret: Bar charts are simple and intuitive. They are easy to read and understand, even for people who are not familiar with data visualization.

3.It can show both the highest and average scores: A bar chart can be used to show both the highest and average scores for each branch. This allows you to see how the top performers compare to the average performers in each branch.

4.It can be customized: Bar charts can be customized to suit your needs. You can change the colors, labels, and other elements to make the chart more visually appealing and informative.
