Pie Chart

A pie chart is a graphical representation technique that displays data in a circular shaped graph.Pie charts are often used to represent sample data with data points belonging to a combination of different categories.

Why have we used Pie chart?

1.Easy to understand: Pie charts are simple and easy to understand. Viewers can quickly interpret the chart and gain an understanding of the distribution of data being presented.

2.Clear representation of proportions: Pie charts are ideal for showing proportions or percentages. Since the whole pie represents 100%, viewers can see at a glance the relative sizes of the different slices, and easily compare the proportions of different categories.

3.Good for comparing categories: Pie charts are useful for comparing categories with each other, as the slices of the pie are all the same shape and size, making it easy to see which categories are larger or smaller than others.

4.Visually appealing: Pie charts can be visually appealing, and can help to engage viewers and draw their attention to the data being presented.
